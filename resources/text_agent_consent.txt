Squire connects to your backend bot via Telegram to process requests. OpenClaw and Hermes are both supported.

Important:
- All your messages and requests are sent through Telegram to your backend instance (OpenClaw or Hermes)
- This app does not control the backend server - it is self-hosted by you or a third party
- Data handling, retention, and privacy depend on your backend configuration
- Your Telegram account and backend bot username are stored locally on this device

By continuing to use Squire, you agree to these terms.
